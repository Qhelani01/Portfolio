Through My Lens

Capturing the beauty of nature, one moment at a time. Here are some of my favorite shots from my adventures in hiking and travel.

Seattle Golden Hour

Captured this stunning sunset over Seattle's skyline during my travels. The way the golden light reflected off the buildings and painted the sky in warm hues was absolutely mesmerizing. This moment reminded me why I love exploring new cities and capturing their unique beauty.

Seattle, WA Recent Travel

Iconic Golden Gate

Standing before the magnificent Golden Gate Bridge was a dream come true. The fog rolling in from the Pacific created this dramatic scene, with the bridge's iconic orange-red color contrasting beautifully against the misty backdrop. This engineering marvel never fails to inspire awe.

San Francisco, CA Recent Travel

Zambezi River Serenity

The Zambezi River flows with such peaceful grace through the African landscape. This moment captured the river's gentle curves and the golden light reflecting off its surface, creating a scene of pure tranquility. It's amazing how water can carve such beautiful paths through the earth over thousands of years.

Zambezi River Recent Travel

California Quail Encounter

This beautiful California Quail was spotted during a peaceful hike in Point Reyes. These birds are known for their distinctive topknot and gentle nature. It was a magical moment watching this little bird go about its day, completely unaware of my presence as I quietly captured this shot.

Point Reyes, CA Recent Hike

Elephant Road Encounter

This magnificent elephant was casually walking down the road in Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe. It was incredible to witness such a powerful animal moving with such grace and confidence through the landscape. The way it carried itself, completely at home in its environment, was a reminder of the beauty and majesty of African wildlife.

Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe Recent Travel

Safari Adventure

This incredible safari moment captured the essence of African wildlife in its natural habitat. The vast landscape, the golden light, and the sense of adventure made this one of the most memorable experiences of my travels. There's something magical about being surrounded by nature's most magnificent creatures in their own world.

Chobe National Park, Botswana Recent Travel
