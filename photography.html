Places that got under my skin. Follow @qhelani_moyo for more.

United States

From Pacific coastlines to mountain light, the West Coast has a way of slowing time. Fog on the Golden Gate, golden hour in Seattle, quiet hikes in Point Reyes—these are the frames I keep coming back to.

Zimbabwe

Victoria Falls doesn’t feel real until you’re there. The spray, the rainbows, the Zambezi rolling off the edge—and elephants walking down the road like they own the place. Tiny weavers building nests in the trees. It gets into your bones.

Botswana

Safari in Chobe is one of those experiences you can’t really explain—you have to stand in the landscape and feel it. The light, the space, and the wildlife moving through it. I’ve never met anyone who regretted going.
